Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using the MediQTrack system.

How can a patient join a queue?

Patients can join a clinic queue by logging in, selecting their preferred clinic, and tapping the “Join Queue” button. They will then be assigned a queue number.

Can patients see their queue number and status in real-time?

Yes. Once a patient joins a queue, they can view their current queue number and status (waiting, in consultation, pharmacy, completed) directly on their homepage.

What should a patient do if they miss their turn?

If a patient misses their turn, they can rejoin the queue, but may be placed at the end depending on clinic policy. Patients are encouraged to stay updated via the app.

How does a clinic manage the queue flow?

Clinics use a web dashboard to view all patients in the queue and update their status from waiting to consultation and pharmacy. This ensures smooth transitions and tracking.

Can clinics pause or close the queue temporarily?

Yes. Clinics can pause new queue entries or close the queue for the day directly from the system dashboard, providing flexibility during peak hours or emergencies.
